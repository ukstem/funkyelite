So.uk Soho

Funky house, done casually.

The exhibit

So.uk Soho shared the famous Shaftesbury Avenue address with CC Club, but ran its own Saturday identity: a new Funky House night, commencing 2004, aimed squarely at a crowd who wanted the music first and the fuss second.

The dress code said it all — trendy, casual, and yes, jeans ok. Warm low-lit rooms, deep seating and a golden Buddha watching over the bar gave So.uk its signature look.
