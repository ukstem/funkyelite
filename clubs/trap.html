Trap

On the site of the old Propaganda Club.

The exhibit

The latest addition to London’s nightlife circuit in its day, Trap took over the site of the old Propaganda Club on Wardour Street after extensive renovations.

Weekends ran in association with Close models, while guest DJs mixed and scratched the best R&B and hip hop tracks in town. The page noted a large gathering of celebrity support at a venue that had already become a sure hit with London’s nightlife culture.

Entry on the guest list: £10 before 11pm, £15 after.
