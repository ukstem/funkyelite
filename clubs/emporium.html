The Emporium

The list expands — Kingly Street, done in style.

A new addition to the list

The Emporium joins the FunkyElite roster as a new Friday-night residency — a room off Kingly Street in the heart of the West End, drawing the same well-turned-out crowd that made Tantra a Saturday institution just a few doors down.

The founding photographs are already in — captured in the room, in the moment, in the FunkyElite frame. See them in the archive strip on the homepage.

More history will be written into this exhibit as the residency establishes itself. For now: the doors are open, the crowd is right, and the camera is watching.

"Same name. Same standard. A whole new chapter."
